About Me

I build experiences,
not just screens.

I'm Ruchitra K, an AI-focused UX/UI designer with a genuine love for design thinking. I earned my Master's in Computer Science from NYIT, and I'm currently pursuing a second Master's at Saint Francis College. With nearly three years of professional experience as a Software Engineer at Ambica Constructions & Contractors, I've worked across the full front-end cycle, designing, coding, testing, and shipping real-world applications. I believe the best interfaces are the ones users don't have to think about.

Outside of code, I volunteer at the 9/11 Memorial, welcoming 1,000+ visitors monthly, and build passion projects like GiftGenie and TaskFlow Pro. I bring the same curiosity and care to every project I work on.
